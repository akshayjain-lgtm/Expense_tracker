Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment card numbers or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.
  • To send transactional emails such as password resets, if applicable.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or unaffiliated third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use the following third-party services to operate the application:

  • Hosting providers — to store and serve the application.
  • Analytics tools — to understand aggregate usage patterns. No individually identifiable data is shared.

We do not integrate with any payment processors, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is strictly necessary for the Service to function and contains no personally identifiable information beyond a session identifier. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app.

For details on the rules governing your use of Spendly, see our Terms and Conditions.
